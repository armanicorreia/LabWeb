What We're About!


The Laboratory was founded in November 2019 by Armani Correia. The mission of The Laboratory is to use the advantages of technology and science to help shape the future and and understand where techology is heading. We do so with the help of applications of subjects such as Computer Science, Networking, Healthcare Informatics, Data Analytics, and various related feilds. Here at the Laboratory, we are working hard to bridge the gaps between society and high technology.

A better future begins today!
