Bio


Dr. Christina Schweikert is an Assistant Professor in the Division of Computer Science, Mathematics and Science. She has been granted a Clare Boothe Luce professorship. Dr. Schweikert completed her Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the City University of New York, Graduate Center and has previously taught at Fordham University and the State University of New York. Dr. Schweikert’s research interests include: programming languages, data mining, knowledge representation, biomedical and healthcare informatics



Research Interests

Data Mining and Analytics, Knowledge Representation, and Biomedical/Healthcare Informatics
